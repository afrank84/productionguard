About Production Guard

Production Guard was born out of necessity and frustration. Created by a lone DevOps engineer who grew weary of the complex backup processes involving GitHub Action runners and intricate scripting, this tool embodies simplicity and efficiency in data protection.

Our mission is to provide a straightforward solution for Jira data backup and storage, ensuring your valuable production information is always protected and easily recoverable. We understand the critical nature of your data and the time constraints you face - that's why we've made Production Guard as user-friendly and automated as possible.

From one developer to another, we're here to make your Jira backup process painless, reliable, and secure.
